About EduHub

EduHub is a simple online learning platform project. It allows students to browse courses, view details, enroll in courses, and track their learning progress in one place.

The platform includes a home page, courses page, course details page, profile page, checkout page, and dashboards for students and instructors.

What the Platform Does

  • Browse and search online courses
  • View detailed information for each course
  • Save favorite courses and enroll in them
  • Track progress from the dashboard
